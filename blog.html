Monsoon Guide

Monsoon Protection Guide: Protect Your POP Ceiling In Mumbai Rain

4 min read

Mumbai monsoons are the biggest enemy of POP ceilings. 70% of all ceiling damage happens during rainy season. Follow these essential tips to protect your investment.

Pre-Monsoon Inspection

Check all corners and edges for any small cracks before rains start.

Keep Windows Closed

Never leave windows open when it rains. Even small moisture will damage POP.

Fix Leaks Immediately

Even one drop of water can cause entire ceiling to fail within 3 days.

Run Dehumidifier

Run AC on dry mode for 1 hour daily during heavy rains.

Important Warning

"If you see any yellow stains appearing on your ceiling - call us immediately. That is water damage starting. Do not wait."

Warnings

7 Mistakes Everyone Makes When Installing POP Ceiling

5 min read

9 out of 10 people make at least one of these mistakes. These mistakes will ruin your ceiling within 2 years and you will have to redo everything completely.

❌ Not using proper sealing coat

This is #1 mistake. Most contractors skip this step to save money.

❌ Using cheap local POP material

Cheap material will crack within 1 year. Always use branded material.

❌ Not enough channels support

They will put channels 4 feet apart instead of 2 feet. Ceiling will sag after 6 months.

❌ No gap at wall edges

Ceiling needs 5mm gap at walls for expansion. Otherwise it will crack.

❌ Using too much water while mixing

Extra water makes POP weaker. Strength reduces by 50%.

❌ Painting immediately

POP needs minimum 7 days drying time before painting. Most contractors paint next day.

❌ Paying full amount in advance

Never pay more than 50% advance. Keep final payment until full satisfaction.
